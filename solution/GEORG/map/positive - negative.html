Advantages electric cars and Disadvantages gasoline cars

Advantages of electric cars:
  1. State support. Buyers of new electric vehicles in Latvia will receive a refund of 4,500 euros
  2. Eco-Friendly. Electric vehicles produce no exhaust fumes or harmful emissions, making them the cleanest and greenest vehicles
  3. Saving money. In Latvia, electricity is cheaper than gasoline or diesel fuel, free parking
  4. Manufacturability. Equipped with the latest technologies - energy recovery systems during braking, allowing you to increase your driving range
  5. Less noise. Electric vehicles operate quietly, making them more comfortable for drivers and passengers
Disadvantages of gasoline cars:
  1. Environmental pollution. Gasoline cars emit harmful substances into the atmosphere, which leads to environmental pollution and negatively affects human health
  2. High fuel costs. Gasoline cars generally have higher fuel consumption than diesel cars
  3. Less Power. Gasoline vehicles may have less power than diesel vehicles
  4. Noisier. Gasoline cars can be noisier than diesel or hybrid cars
  5. Higher Maintenance Costs. Gasoline vehicles may require more frequent maintenance and repairs, which can result in additional costs
